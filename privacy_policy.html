Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and shared when you use the Board Game Inventory app.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: The app does not collect personal data unless explicitly provided by the user (e.g., loan records).
  • Device Information: Information such as device type and operating system may be collected for app functionality.
  • Camera Access: Used for barcode scanning. No images are stored or transmitted.
  • Storage Access: Used for importing/exporting game data and saving images locally.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide app functionality (e.g., barcode scanning, data storage).
  • Improve user experience and app performance.
  • Enable import/export features for user data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying ads. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RapidAPI: For barcode lookups. Refer to their privacy policy for details.

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on the device. No data is transmitted to external servers unless explicitly requested by the user (e.g., barcode lookups).

Your Rights

  • You can delete your data by uninstalling the app.
  • You can manage permissions (e.g., camera, storage) through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at support@boardgameinventory.com.
